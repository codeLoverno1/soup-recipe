Easy Homemade Vegetable Soup

You Will Need

  1. 3 tablespoons olive oil
  2. 2 cups chopped onion (1 medium)
  3. 1 ½ cups chopped carrot (3 to 4 carrots)
  4. 1 ½ cups chopped celery (2 to 3 stalks)
  5. 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  6. 4 teaspoons minced garlic (4 cloves)
  7. 3/4 teaspoon ground fennel seed
  8. 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  9. 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt, plus more to taste
  10. 1/8 to 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional for heat)
  11. 1 (15-ounce) can diced tomatoes with their liquid
  12. 6 cups stock
  13. 2 cups chopped potato (2 medium)
  14. 3 to 4 heaped cups chopped or shredded cabbage
  15. 2 bay leaves
  16. 1 cup frozen peas
  17. 1/2 teaspoon apple cider vinegar or fresh lemon juice, optional
